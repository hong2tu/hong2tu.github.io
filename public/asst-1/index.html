Skincare

  1. Cleanser

    Facial care product used to remove make-up, dead skin cells, oil, dirt, and any other types of pollutants from the skin of the face.
    • Oil based: Cleansing balm to clear skin of make-up, and SPF. Good for make-up removing defense!
    • Water based: Used to remove water based impurities like sweat, and dirt.
    • Foaming: Used to wash oil, other kinds of make-up, and also stubborn SPF.

  2. Moisturizer

    Facial care product used to prevent extreme dryness, or oiliness which can cause acne, and other skin blemishes.
    • Humectants: Absorbs water, and hydrates the top layer of the skin.
    • Occlusives: Help prevent dehydration of the top layer of skin.
    • Emollients: Fills cracks of skin to keep it moisturized, and hydrated.

  3. Sunscreen

    Facial care product used to remove make-up, dead skin cells, oil, dirt, and any other types of pollutants from the skin of the face.
    • Premature aging: UV radiation causes breakdown of collagen, and elastin in skin cells.
    • Reduces the risk of Hyperpigmentation: when skin darkens due to changes in production of melanin.
    • Prevents inflammation of the skin: UV radiation damages immune-protecting cells, which can be damaged without SPF.
